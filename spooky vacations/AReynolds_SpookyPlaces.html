A Bone-Chilling Getaway!

Ghost City Inn

Jerome, Arizona


Ghost City Inn was built in the 1890s as a boarding house for miners, but was later used for a variety of things, such as an ashram and a restaurant. It is home to a female spirit that is most often seen in the Cleopatra Hill Home. There are also claims of a male spirit lurking through the halls. In addition to these sightings, you may also hear doors slamming, and strange voices when you are in a room alone.



There are many other things you can do in Jerome when you feel you've been cooped up in your room. In this ghost town, there's never a dull moment! Here's a list of activities in the area:

  • Visit the Tuzigoot National Monument if you are interested in the historical sights.
  • Nature lovers can spend a day in the Dead Horse Ranch State Park.
  • View the Raku Gallery if you are a fan of art.
  • Hike the Mingus Mountain if you're looking to be more active.
